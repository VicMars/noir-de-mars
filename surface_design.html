Surface Design

“I don’t believe in the power of colour.
I believe in the power of shapes and forms.
That is what intrigues me, what inspires me.
Always searching for new structures or new textures,
I design patterns as unexpected surfaces to explore.
That is why my work process always starts with geometric shapes.
Relentlessly, I reinvent, reconstruct or rearrange them to create an
abstract world where everything is possible.”
