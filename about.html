I am a French designer based in Montreal, Canada.
After studying and teaching Visual Arts in Paris, I quickly felt the need to put my creative mind to more concrete applications. Fascinated by contemporary art and intrigued by the power of fashion, it is quite naturally that I trained in leather crafts and textile design. Interested in the tactility of things, my artistic universe is built primarily through my travels and urban wanderings, transforming photography and geometry into visual textures.
Now teaching textile design, I combine several activities: my blog Collectif Textile, created in 2012 with my friends and associates to promote contemporary textile design; and my print design studio called ct•studio.
